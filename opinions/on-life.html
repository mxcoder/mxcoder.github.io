My current opinions

The world is a mess, politics are shit, democracy is at peril, economy is tough, capitalism is thriving, war is hell.

And still, I fight back, not as a virtue signal, but as a true burning desire to feel alive.

As days go by, I try to rage, and enrage others.

The best think you can do with your time is spend it with others.

Talk to others, in real life if possible, at least video call your loved ones, at least once per week, listen to what they do and feel.

Nobody has all the answers, nobody will. You must find your own ways and coping mechanism. Let others help you.

Work is work is work. Companies are not families, teams can be friends.

Social networks were a huge mistake.
